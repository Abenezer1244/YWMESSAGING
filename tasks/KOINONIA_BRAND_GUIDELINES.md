**Minimum Clear Space:**

## üéØ TONE OF VOICE

### Brand Personality

**Professional yet Warm**
- Trustworthy and reliable
- Approachable and friendly
- Clear and direct
- Respectful of church values

**Communication Style:**
- Use "we" and "you" (not "our platform")
- Focus on benefits, not features
- Speak to church administrators directly
- Use church-friendly language

### Example Messaging

**Good:**
- "Connect every member, every time"
- "Built for churches like yours"
- "Strengthen your church community"

**Avoid:**
- Technical jargon
- Overly salesy language
- Generic corporate speak
